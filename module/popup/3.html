Thumbnail TIP

  • Sometimes thumbnails are not just the same image shrunk down. Try cropping out an interesting part of the larger image, and shrink that image down for the thumb. The mixer is just a shrunk thumbnail of entire image. The lion is an example of a "crppped" thumbnail. View in separate window
  • TIP: Name your files mixer.jpg and mixer_t.jpg for easy programming. As you add more thumbs, make sure classes and id's are unique as shown in this example.
